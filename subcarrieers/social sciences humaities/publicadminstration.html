Public Administration

Summary

Public Administration deals with the aim to study development, organization and implementation of government policies and prepare civil servants for their work. The understanding of this subject helps in increasing the efficiency of human, financial and material resources, and also helps governmental institutions become more efficient and organized.

Career Opportunities in Public Administration

  • Administrative Officers
    A wide range of government occupations, from civil servants to legislature to intelligence bodies, are available for those with a major in Public Administration. Administrators in Central, State and Local Government are engaged in administration and management of functions of the Government.
  • Consultants
    The consultants conduct research relating to budgeting and financial analysis, public relations or marketing, shaping organizational policy, program development and implementation. Consultants may be employed in public or private sectors who lead different organizations.
  • Teaching Professions
    Employment opportunities for individuals with public administration majors are available at the school level and at the college level.
  • Corporate Manager
    Corporate Managers are employed by government agencies, small businesses, health care and educational institutions. As a public administrator graduate is trained in these skills, he may seek positions in organizational management or programme management in the public or private sector.

Pros & Cons of a Career in Public Administration

Pros
  • Great option for entering into fields like Law, Politics, Government services etc.
  • Helps in critically analyse and review public policies of the government.
  • Scoring subject and a good foundation course for government services
Cons
  • Not a professional course
  • Needs administrative skills to be sharp
  • Needs Master’s degree for good job opportunities
