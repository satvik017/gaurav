Digital Marketing

Summary

Digital marketing is a fast-expanding field with excellent opportunities to build a career. Using various media vehicles such as e-commerce, mobile commerce, etc. they promote the organization’s products and brands. Digital marketing has become a critical pillar for an organizations marketing department as it enables the organization to analyze marketing campaigns and understand what is working and what isn’t typically in real time.

Career Opportunities in Digital Marketing

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
    Search engine optimization is a technique, or strategy to increase the number of visitors to a website by obtaining a high-ranking placement in the search result pages of a search engine (SERP) including Google, Yahoo, Bing, Firefox, and others.
  • Email Marketing
    It is also known as direct marketing wherein commercial message is sent to a group of people using email. Unlike spam, direct email marketing reaches those target audience to inform them about company?-?s products and services.
  • Mobile Marketing
    Mobile marketing is marketing on or with a mobile device, such as a cell phone. It is any marketing activity conducted through a ubiquitous network to which consumers are constantly connected using a personal mobile device.
  • Social Media Marketing
    It is a form of Internet marketing which is done so as to increase the brand exposure and broaden customer reach. Various social networking websites are used as a marketing tool, such as Facebook, twitter, Instagram, etc.
  • Content Marketing
    Content marketing is a strategic marketing approach focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly-defined audience ?-" and, ultimately, to drive profitable customer action. Content marketing?-?s purpose is to attract and retain customers by consistently creating and curating relevant and valuable content with the intention of changing or enhancing consumer behavior.
  • Affiliate Marketing
    It is a type of performance-based marketing in which a business rewards one or more affiliates for each visitor or customer brought by the affiliate's own marketing efforts.

Important Facts

If you wish to make a career in the field of Digital Marketing, it is not advisable to pursue a full-time course from renowned institutes, as this field is constantly updating. In this field, short-term online certification courses hold great value, which can be pursued alongside your higher education or job. You can get certification in various fields such as- Google Analytics, SEO, Social Media management.

Work Description

  • Design, build and maintain the presence in the social media to reach the target audience
  • Develop, plan and execute digital marketing campaigns such as web campaigns, SEO/SEM, database marketing and display advertising campaigns
  • Sell the organization's products and services using the online or mobile platform to enhance market share
  • Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns, and assess target goals
  • Coordinate with internal teams to create landing pages of websites and optimize the overall user experience
  • Utilize strong analytical ability to evaluate end-to-end customer experience across multiple channels and customer touch points
  • Use web analytics software to monitor performance of customers on the web or mobile platform and make recommendations for improvement

Pros & Cons of a Career in Digital Marketing

Pros
  • Its reach is vast so you can impact the lives of mass audience.
  • Opportunity to earn good money, as one can work in an organization as well as a freelancer.
Cons
  • Lot of competition present in the industry.
  • Requires a lot of patience, convincing a client about your idea takes a lot of effort, time and money
