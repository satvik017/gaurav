Hotel Management

Summary

Hotel Management is an upcoming global industry & has multifocussed approach. It is flourishing in big as well as small cities. It prepares students to work at hotels, resorts, cruise liners, in aviation and casinos. At hotels, hotel management graduates may engage in managing functions such as housekeeping, front office, food production and administration. Hotel Management aspirants can specialize in fields like Hospitality Marketing Strategy, Culinary Theory and Practice, Hotel Operations, Hospitality Franchising, Hospitality Technology & Financial Analysis in Hospitality Industry.

Career Opportunities in Hotel Management

  • Hotel/ Restaurant Industry
    Be part of the leading 5 star hotels/ restaurants chains in the country and be responsible for managing multiple departments such as Operations, Conference/ Banquets, Culinary Arts, Front Office, Food & Production, Spa, Housekeeping etc.
  • Cruise Liners
    Cruise Liners are emerging to be the most sought after destination amongst Hotel Management Graduates. Opportunities in Cruise Lines are characterized by high pay packages and exotic destinations.
  • Airline Industry
    One can join the airline industry and be part of the cabin crew or ground staff. Lucrative opportunities exist in both domestic airlines & international airlines.
  • Retail Industry
    Be part of the team, which manages the leading malls & cineplexes in the country.
  • Corporate Roles
    Be part of the leading corporate firms and be responsible for functions such as Guest Relations, Customer Service & Public Relations

Work Description

  • Coordinate with and manage different departments for the smooth running of the hotel
  • Handle the financial matters and look after the expenditure of the hotel
  • Look after the maintenance of the hotel
  • Work towards providing highest quality of services to the clients
  • Recruit, train and monitor the staff
  • Do periodic assessment of the hotel and its departments
  • Come up with quick solutions to problems to avoid the inconvenience to the clients
  • Maintain the harmony among staff members
  • Set a good example to the staff members which will motivate them to work efficiently
  • Take care of the ethical concerns while governing a hotel
  • Work on the feedbacks provided by the customers as well as other staff members

Pros & Cons of a Career in Hotel Management

Pros
  • Working in the hotel industry gives us an advantage of meeting new people.
  • It helps you in developing your over all personality
  • With a hotel management degree you are not just restricted to work in hotels, but also in event management, airlines, travel and tourism, etc.
Cons
  • There are no fixed working hours.
  • Starting salary can be low
