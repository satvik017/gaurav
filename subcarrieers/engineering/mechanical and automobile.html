Mechanical and Automobile Engineering

Summary

Mechanical Engineers are solution providers for development of processes and products that range from tiny component designs to mega manufacturing plants. Likewise, automobile engineers design, facilitate and develop designs of vehicles like cars and motorbikes. Coming from very diverse disciplines of engineering, mechanical/automobile engineers can work right from inception stage, R&D of their product to its final commissioning. They take care of the mechanical, electronic, electric, software and safety elements during the manufacturing.

Career Opportunities in Mechanical and Automobile Engineering

  • Design
    Mechanical Engineers draft technical drawings, manually or with the aid of computers.
  • Production
    Mechanical Engineers supervise the manufacturing of mechanical components and machines.
  • Analysis and testing
    Mechanical Engineers analyze and test different types of machines and their parts to ensure that they function flawlessly.
  • Installation
    Mechanical Engineers install machines and mechanical parts at their client's location.
  • Maintenance
    Mechanical Engineers primarily ensure that machinery is working as per specifications.
  • Maintenance of Aircraft or Missiles
    Mechanical Engineers with specialization in Aircraft technology, test new aerodynamic and aviation systems and maintain them.
  • Structural engineering
    Mechanical Engineers help create structural elements of design, for bridges & skyscrapers and also design and assess major civil projects, such as buildings, bridges, or dams, to ensure their strength and durability.
  • Aesthetic Compatibility
    Mechanical Engineers design machines keeping in mind the aesthetic compatibility of the design with its use.

How to Pursue a Career in Mechanical and Automobile Engineering

Stream Graduation After Graduation After Post Graduation
Path 1
 Clear Class XII with Science Stream with Maths Pursue B.Tech in Mechanical/ Automobile Engineering for 4 years Pursue M.Tech for 2-3 years Pursue Ph.D for 2-3 years
Path 2
 Clear Class XII with Science Stream with Maths Pursue B.Tech + M.Tech (Dual Degree)in Mechanical/ Automobile Engineering for 5 years - Pursue Ph.D for 2-3 years

Important Facts

  • Most colleges have eligibility criteria of minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class XII and some of minimum 60%
  • Must have passed class 12thor senior secondary examination with at least 50% marks including English
  • Some exams require JEE score as minimum eligibility
  • Some colleges/ universities also have an age limit
  • Mechanical & Automobile Engineering is available as a course only in select colleges.
  • Specializations are also available in select colleges.

Work Description

  • Perform engineering activities in planning and designing tools, engines and other mechanically functioning equipments
  • Develop, test and evaluate theoretical designs
  • Discuss and solve complex problems with manufacturing departments, sub-contractors, suppliers and customers
  • Monitor and commission plant and systems
  • Develop, coordinate, or monitor all aspects of production or operation of product designs
  • Investigate into equipment failures and recommend remedial measures
  • Develop or test models of alternate designs or processing methods to assess feasibility, sustainability, operating conditions, potential new applications, or necessity of modification
  • Research and analyse customers designing proposals, manuals, or other data to evaluate the, cost, or maintenance requirements of designs or applications
  • Estimate costs or submit bids for engineering, construction, or extraction works

Pros & Cons of a Career in Mechanical and Automobile Engineering

Pros
  • Learn how to manipulate and build machines.
  • Offers extensive learning and growth as it encompasses many other fields.
  • Interesting to study and apply as well.
Cons
  • Initial Pay Package may not be as lucrative as other fields.
  • The subjects are tough to master as level of technicality is high.
  • Have to remain constantly updated on newer technology.
