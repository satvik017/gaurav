Sales

Summary

Sales is a core function of every business across industries. People involved in this profession reach out to their target customers to show their products or services. This profession involves a lot travelling, meeting new people, planning stratergies and close the deals to achieve the company target. Career in sales allows one to work across almost any industry or domain.

Career Opportunities in Sales

  • Pharmaceutical Industry
    Sales people employed by pharmaceutical companies informs doctors about the drugs avaialable in their company and pursue them to use their companies medicine. Their work is to generate sale revenue by providing right product to the right doctors.
  • Automobile Industry
    A salesperson in the automobile industry work with the manufacturers who deals with cars, commercial vehicles and other product related to automobile industry. Their task involves understanding the customer needs and specific requirements and guide them towards the most appropriate product.
  • FMCG Industry
    Retail industry where same or similar products are offered to the customers, a good sales person can make all the difference. A success or failure of a product is dependent on how well prepared a sales person is, and can gain maximum benefit from the product featured.
  • Retail Industry
    People who pursue careers in retail are required to engage with the customers and explain the benefit of the merchandise in order to help them make an informed decision. Their objective is to improve sales figures and boost revenue.
  • Information Technology Industry
    This industry is particularly suitable for people with a background of IT. Sales professionals in this industry liaison with technical and purchasing staff to understand clients IT related requirements.
  • Finance & Banking Industry
    Sales professionals in this sector would be selling a range of products such as pensions, life insurance, mortgages and saving schemes, as either an independent financial advisor or as a representative of a financial institution.
  • Real State Industry
    They are engaged in work such as like renting, buying, or selling property for clients. Perform duties such as interviewing prospective clients, studying property listings, accompanying clients to property site, discussing conditions of sale, and drawing up real estate contracts.

Work Description

  • Act as a contact between a company and its existing and potential markets
  • Pitch for products and offerings of a company to customers
  • Travel extensively to meet and interact with customers
  • Monitor competition by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, new products, delivery schedules, merchandising techniques, etc.
  • Create detailed proposal documents, often as part of a formal bidding process dictated by the prospective customer
  • Recommend changes in products, services, and policy by evaluating results and competitive developments.
  • Resolve customers complaints by investigating into problems, developing solutions, preparing reports, making recommendations to management.
  • Maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending to educational workshops and participating in professional societies.
  • Negotiate the terms of an agreement and closing sales

Pros & Cons of a Career in Sales

Pros
  • You get to interact with a lot of people, that helps you widen your network.
  • There are good travel oppurtunities
  • There are lot of perks such as gift cards, invites, etc.
Cons
  • There is high pressure.
  • You are required to deal with difficult customers.
