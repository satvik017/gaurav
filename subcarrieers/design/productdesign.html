Product Design

Summary

Product design is a subset of Industrial design. They use their designing skills and technical knowledge to design new products or improve the way that existing product look or work. Product designer designs product keeping in mind the consumer market and are involved in the whole product design life cycle, from concept to its design delivery.

Career Opportunities in Product Design

  • Exhibition Designer
    They work in large exhibitions, trade shows, conferences, museum, (public and private organization) and create display and fixtures during show. They are responsible for overseeing the supplies.
  • Furniture Designer
    They are responsible for producing design for items of furniture and related items. They study, and design various style of furniture design.
  • Product/ Industrial design researcher
    They work closely with the marketing department and are involved in understanding the needs of the user and come up with solutions and suggestions for the product being used at the user’s end.
  • Consulting Firms
    They work with firms and help choose all the elements of design as per the need of approaching clients.

Work Description

  • Sketching the Initial design ideas.
  • Meet clients to discuss design brief and make samples for the same.
  • Maintain an awareness of current design trends and influences
  • Correcting Product faults, be accurate and pay attention to detail
  • working on ideas as part of a team or developing design concepts using CAD (computer-aided design)
  • Arrange meetings and liaison with engineers and marketing department to discuss and negotiate appropriate production processes, costs and commercial issues
  • Evaluate the safety of the product, and functionality of products

Pros

  • It is a high paying and high demand career
Cons
  • It can be a stressful job since it needs perfection
  • It requires a high level of study and skill
