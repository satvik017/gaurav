Graphic Design

Summary

A designer who creates visual impact using computer-software are graphic designers. They understand the needs and requirements of their clients before making design decisions. They work for various products and activities, such as websites, advertising, books, magazines, posters, computer games, product packaging, exhibitions, etc. The key skills required include creative flair, up-to-date knowledge of industry software and a professional approach to time, costs and deadlines.

Career Opportunities in Graphic Design

  • Online and Print Publishing Companies
    Graphic designers play an important role in Online & Print Publishing Companies.
  • Graphic Design Studios
    Most of the graphic designers work in Graphic Design Studios.
  • Corporates - Marketing/ Corporate Communication
    Graphic Designers are in huge demand in the corporate sector. They are employed by top MNC's.
  • Advertising Companies
    Advertising companies hire graphic designers and they are responsible for the visual style and images in magazines, newspapers, product packaging, and movie, etc. They create the overall design of a project.
  • Freelancers
    After gaining some experience in the field, graphic designers can start their work as freelancers.
  • UI/UX Designers
    Graphic designers work as UI/UX designers in various IT firms.
  • Web/ Game Designing Studios
    Graphic designers design games and form the core part of the operations.

How to Become a Graphic Designer

Stream Graduation After Graduation After Post Graduation
Path 1
 Class XII with Any Stream (Science for some) Pursue Bachelors in Design (Graphics) for 3-4 years Pursue Masters in Design (Graphics) for 2 years -
Path 2
 Clear Class XII with any stream Pursue Diploma in Graphic Design for 6 months to 2 years. Pursue PG Diploma in Graphic Design for 1-2 years. -
Path 3
 Clear Class XII Any Stream Pursue graduation in any field. Pursue Post Graduate Diploma in Design/Graphic Design for 1-2 years -

Important Facts

  • The candidate must have secured minimum 50% in class XII.
  • For Design courses at IIT's one must have Science with Maths in Class XII.
  • Most colleges have an age limit of 20 years.

Work Description

  • Combine art and technology to create images, logos, websites, advertisements, etc.
  • Understand the needs of the client and then create designs accordingly
  • Discuss the planned layout with art directors and the clients before implementing it
  • Work with different software programmes
  • Take decisions regarding the font, colour combination and other animations to make the product look attractive to the viewers
  • Review the designs with the client and make the necessary amendments
  • Promote an organisation's agenda by reaching out to the masses
  • Use one's creativity and innovation to produce unique illustrations

Pros & Cons of a Career in Graphic Design

Pros
  • Graphic Designers are employed in multiple industries like web designing, print industry, etc.
  • There is a huge demand.
  • You can work as a freelancer with multiple companies.
Cons
  • It is important to learn various programs for success in the industry.
  • The competition is high in the industry.
  • You should be very creative.
