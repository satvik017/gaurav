Web Design

Summary

Web designing is the process of the production and maintenance of the layout, colors, text styles, structure, graphics of the websites, etc. using both non-technical and technical skills. They are involved in producing not just the look of the website, but they determine its working as well. Their main role is to generate the look and the feel of the website through integrating multiple forms of media.

Career Opportunities in Web Design

  • Online and Print Publishing Companies
    Online publishing companies employ web designers to help in designing portals.
  • Web/ Game Designing Studios
    Web designers play a key role in Web/Game designing studios.
  • Corporates - Marketing/ Corporate Communication
    With the increase in the importance of internet, the demand of Web designers has increased in the corporate sector.
  • Advertising Companies
    Advertising companies hire web designers.
  • Freelancers
    Most of the web designers work as freelancers.
  • Graphic Design Studios
    Web designers can also work in graphic design studios.

Work Description

  • Design web pages in accordance with the clients, need and target audience
  • Create wireframes, user flows, site maps to enable design and development of apps and websites
  • Take decisions regarding the layout, fonts, colour combinations and the content
  • Keep in touch with the clients for reviews and possible editions
  • Make sure that the web page is appropriate for the target audience
  • Ensure the captivity power of the web page
  • Ensure that the website is easy to use and access
  • Verify the content of the website for authentication
  • One should be able to use innovation in creation of user-friendly designs

Pros & Cons of a Career in Web Design

Pros
  • You can work as a freelancer.
  • There is a huge demand of website designers.
  • You can work on multiple projects at a time.
Cons
  • You will be required to work for longer hours.
  • You should be up-to-date with the latest software.
  • It is a complete desk job.
