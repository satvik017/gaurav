UX Design

Summary

User experience is how a person feels when interfacing with a website, a web application or desktop software. UX study and evaluate how users feel about a system. They are responsible for ensuring ease of use and efficiency in performing tasks. They work on the aesthetics, utility, and efficiency. They strive to give as much detail as possible in order to enhance an individual's experience while interacting with a software.

Career Opportunities in UX Design

  • Web/ App Development Companies
    UX designers work in high demand in Web/App development companies.
  • Online and Print Publishing Companies
    Online and print publishing companies employ UX designers.
  • Graphic Design Studios
    UX designers also work in graphic design studios.
  • Corporates - Marketing/ Corporate Communication
    Corporate sector has many jobs for UX designers.
  • Advertising Companies
    UX designers play an important role in advertising companies.
  • Freelancers
    UX designers work as freelancers.

Work Description

  • Produce an interface for human-computer interaction
  • Work in team with different designers to maximize user experience in an app or website
  • Focus on the working of the product and the user experience
  • Create the product keeping in mind the psychology of the users along with latest market trends
  • Use different computer software programmes
  • Create wireframes, user flows, site maps to enable design and development of apps and websites
  • Review the designed product and make the necessary amendments

Pros & Cons of a Career in UX Design

Pros
  • A person from any stream can pursue this field.
  • UX designing has immense scope and high salaries.
  • You will get the opportunity to work with clients and understand human behaviour.
Cons
  • It is a highly competitive field.
  • You should be aware of the latest software.
