UI Design

Summary

The User Interface (UI) designer's responsibility is to take care of the overall experience that visitors to a site will have. This will include layout, presentation, and navigation. Being a UI designer involves a great deal of interaction with marketing and other departments. UI designer works to present right corporate image. Your aesthetic skills and knowledge should be high. Your major role as a UI designer is to make sure that the site is clear, concise, and easy to use.

Career Opportunities in UI Design

  • Web/ App Development Companies
    You can work with the Web/App development companies. They focus a lot on customer experience the most and UI designers take care of this part.
  • Online and Print Publishing Companies
    Online & Print Publishing Companies hire UI designers.
  • Graphic Design Studios
    UI designers work in graphic studios to help graphic designers to provide best customer experience.
  • Corporates - Marketing/ Corporate Communication
    UI designers are in huge demand in corporate sector these days.
  • Advertising Companies
    Advertising agencies employ UI designers.
  • Freelancers
    You can work with different companies as a freelancer.

Work Description

  • Execute the visual design stages of an app or website development from concept to final development
  • Think of ideas that bring simplicity and user friendliness to complex designs
  • Analyse user data to make changes in the design to enhance user experience
  • Coordinate and work with a team of designers while developing an app or a website
  • Create wireframes, user flows, site maps to enable design and development of apps and websites
  • Conduct user research and evaluate user feedback
  • Review the designed product and make the necessary amendments
  • Use different computer software programmes

Pros & Cons of a Career in UI Design

Pros
  • Job opportunities are increasing.
  • UX designing is an impactful job.
  • You can work peacefully at your desk.
Cons
  • You will be required to do the same kind of work, which might become boring over time.
  • The working hours can be long.
  • There is a high competition in the area.
