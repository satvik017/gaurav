Acting/Dramatics

Summary

Actors imbibe the characters they play, and become the face of the production. They are the ones who bring the vision of the director to life, and stun the audience with their performance. Be it the theatre, films, or television, actors over the due course of time, become a brand themselves.

Career Opportunities in Acting/Dramatics

  • Television
    An actor can work in TV serials.
  • Movies
    An actor can act in Movies and showcase his/her skills.
  • Theatre
    Performing in a theatre on stage is the biggest challenge for an actor as he has to perform in front of a live audience and the option of retakes isn't present.

Important Facts

Drama/Acting Industry isn't a degree specific industry. A degree or a course in acting helps in honing your acting, but a degree isn't a pre- requisite.
Important Qualities
Dedication, Discipline, Interpersonal skills, Acting talent, Physical stamina, Promotional skills.

Work Description

  • Portray different roles and characters for entertainment purposes
  • Work as a part of a film, television, theatre or drama
  • Direct and produce drama for the live audience
  • Understand the mood, feeling and emotion of the character before acting
  • Captivate the viewers by generating diverse emotions such as humour, anger, love, etc.
  • Understand the storyline and the script to form a contextual basis for the character
  • Provide voice-over in advertisements, animated movies, radio, etc
  • Gain popularity by developing skills of dancing and singing
  • Look attractive through good health and personality
  • Practice and rehearse for several hours for a refined performance

Pros & Cons of a Career in Acting/Dramatics

Pros
  • You get to live different characters.
  • You get to travel different destination as a part of your job.
  • This is a field wherein you earn well from the begning itself.
  • The fame you get in this industry can not be matched with any other industry.
Cons
  • Being a part of this field, your life will be scrutanised all the time.
  • As there is a lot of competition in this path, you need to have a lot of resilience and perseverance.
  • You have to follow a very hectic schedule.
