Modelling

Summary

Modeling is one of the most glamourous professions in the world. With so many products being launched everyday, it has become indespensible part of companies to promote their products and hence use models to promotion. Fashion models work in various fields, including photographic modeling, television commercials, live or runway modeling, and product modeling. In short, Models pose for artists, photographers, or customers to help advertise a variety of products, including clothing, cosmetics, food, and appliances

Career Opportunities in Modelling

  • Fashion Shows
    In the fashion show models walk on a ramp to display clothes in front of an audience.
  • Photographic Modelling
    In photographic, advertising and commercial modelling models work in a studio or various locations. They take direction from a director, photographers in order to pose, act or deliver lines in TV commercials.
  • In House Live Model
    In an in-house live modeling, the designer fits garments onto the model during the ongoing workshop and showcase the finished clothing to fashion buyers and private customers.

Important Facts

  • No formal education is required and training is limited
  • Specific requirements depend on the client
  • However, most models must be within certain ranges for height, weight, and clothing size to meet the needs of fashion designers, photographers, and advertisers

Important Qualities

  • Dedication
  • Discipline
  • Interpersonal skills
  • Confidence
  • Physical stamina
  • Promotional skills

Work Description

  • Promote different products through advertisements and commercials
  • Display fashion through designer clothing in fashion shows
  • Pose for photographers and artists
  • Put on view accessories such as handbags, jewellery, shoes, clothing, make up, hair styles, etc.
  • Maintain appearance by remaining fit and in shape
  • Maintain one's portfolio of the work that has been done so far
  • Indulge in social networking to meet and build with the clients
