Music

Summary

Musicians create music. They compose, conduct, sing, perform music in different styles. They are skilled artists who dedicate their life to music and its composition by playing a variety of instruments. They may work on thier own, as freelancers or in collaboration with others. Competition in the field of music is high and musicians need to dedicate hours of practice to maintain and develop their skill, whatever their preferred style.

Career Opportunities in Music

  • Musician or Singer
    Musicians compose music and play various musical instruments. A musician can be a composer, singer, lyricist while Singers on the other hand gives voice to the lyrics. Both singers and musicians perform in a live performance (concerts) or in the recordings for films, theatre, etc.
  • Music Teacher
    Music teachers has an adequate knowledge in the field of music. He/she teachers students how to play various intruments as well as gives singing lessons. Music teachers must have a bachelor's degree in music education.
  • Musical Director or Composer
    Musical directors selects and plans music for groups such as orchestras, bands, or choirs during live group performances. A music composer creates composition for movies, albums, etc as per the vision communicated by the music director.
  • Arranger
    An Arranger works closely with the writer. He does the creative mixing and aims at bringing out what the writer was trying to convey when he wrote the composition
  • Lyricist
    A lyricist gives words to the music composed.
  • Music Editor
    Music Editor makes sure that the music is in tempo with the scenes and enchances the feel of the video being watched.
  • Music Therapist
    Using many methods, from singing and composing to rhythmic movement or playing instruments,music therapists incorporate melody and rhythm into their patients' treatment.
  • Freelancer
    Working as a freelancer is the latest trend in Musician Industry. Freelancers don't work for a particular company and aren't bounded by any contracts of any recording studio or production house.

Work Description

  • Compose music for recording in a studio or perform in front of live audience
  • Practice and rehearse regularly to maintain the melody in the voice
  • Gain expertise in different genres of music as well as in playing instrument(s)
  • Promote one's music through internet or by performing at different venues
  • Work as a single or in a band, chorus or orchestra
  • Guide the upcoming talents with one's own knowledge and experience
  • Produce music for the film industry
  • Be self-confident and highly motivated to overcome the struggling period

Pros & Cons of a Career in Music

Pros
  • It fulfils your passion for music. Music as a therapy is also used on people, so you can also serve the society.
  • Being good at your work allows you to do play back singing for famous personalities.
  • You get fame and money.
Cons
  • This industry being unpredictable, musicians have to deal with part-time work.
  • There is a cut throat competition in this industry thus you have to work very hard.
